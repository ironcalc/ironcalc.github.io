Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

At IronCalc, we believe that your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit ironcalc.com and use the IronCalc spreadsheet engine.

1. Data Controller

IronCalc is an open-source project. For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the data controller is:

IronCalc Team
Email: hello@ironcalc.com

2. We Do Not Collect Your Spreadsheet Data

IronCalc is designed to run locally in your browser via WebAssembly (WASM) or on your own hardware.

Local Processing: When you use the IronCalc app, your data, formulas, and files are processed locally on your device.

No Server Uploads: We do not upload your spreadsheet content to our servers. We cannot see, access, or store the data you input into IronCalc.

3. Information We Collect

Because we value your privacy, we collect the absolute minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve our service.

A. Technical Logs (Server Side)

When you access our website, our hosting provider may automatically collect and store technical information in server log files. This includes:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access
  • The URL of the page requested

Legal Basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate Interest). This data is used solely for ensuring the security and stability of our website.

B. Communication Data

If you contact us via email (e.g., hello@ironcalc.com) or participate in our GitHub discussions, we will process the information you provide (name, email address, message content) to respond to your inquiry.

Legal Basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (Performance of a contract/pre-contractual steps) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (Legitimate interest).

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, marketing cookies, or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) on ironcalc.com.

We may use "session storage" or "local storage" in your browser strictly for functional purposes (e.g., remembering your application state), which does not involve the transfer of personal data to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

  • We host our code and manage community interactions on GitHub. When you interact with our repository, GitHub's privacy policy applies to the data they collect.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: You can ask us to delete your data.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your data based on legitimate interests.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@ironcalc.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a Data Protection Authority in the EU.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top will indicate when changes were made.
